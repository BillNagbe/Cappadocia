Cappadocia

Welcomes you!

Cappadocia, is thought to have been occupied around (499bce) during the Ionian Revolt, according to Herodotus. "Cappadocia" is a collection of regions including Nevsehir, Kayseri, Aksaray, Kirsehir, Sivas and Nigde. Early Chirstians heavy influenced the region, from the many churches(thousands), to the underground living spaces found throughout due to the Chirstians using the region as an area to escape persecution. Today the region is mostly known for it's beautiful sights and tourism, but we must not forget the rich history the region has.

The region is located southwest of Kayseri, with public transportation to Istanbul, and other major cities. Some of the great sites to visit in the Nevsehir region. Nevsehir: has been declared a world peace city by the United Nations in 2024.
